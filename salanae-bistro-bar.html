Our Story

Where Indochine
Flavours Come Alive

Salanae Bistro & Bar is a beacon of hospitality in the heart of downtown Vientiane — a vibrant gathering place where the warmth of Laotian culture meets modern, stylish social dining.

Our kitchen celebrates the depth and complexity of Indochine cuisine: bespoke exotic dishes crafted with fresh local ingredients, alongside international favourites that honour every palate. Pair them with signature cocktails inspired by the flavours and botanicals of Laos.

Whether you arrive for a leisurely dinner, a lively Ladies' Night, or simply to share drinks with friends, Salanae's welcoming ambience makes every moment memorable.

The Experience

More Than a Meal —
A Destination

Every visit to Salanae is crafted around three pillars: extraordinary food, creative cocktails, and an atmosphere that invites you to linger.

🍜

Indochine Cuisine

From delicate Laotian delicacies to international favourites, our kitchen bridges tradition and innovation. Local ingredients, global inspiration — every dish tells a story.

🍸

Signature Cocktails

Our bartenders craft bespoke cocktails using local spirits and fresh botanicals. Classic pours alongside unique creations that showcase the aromatic soul of Laos.

✨

Stylish Ambience

Modern aesthetics woven with traditional Laotian touches. Warm lighting, outdoor seating, and live music nights create a vibrant social scene unlike anywhere in Vientiane.
